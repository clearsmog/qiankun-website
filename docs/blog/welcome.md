# Welcome to My Blog

Hello and welcome! This is the first post on my new website at **qiankun.co.uk**.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

- **Technology**: New tools, frameworks, and techniques I'm exploring
- **Development**: Tips, tutorials, and lessons from real-world projects
- **Thoughts**: Reflections on the industry and where things are heading

## Why Start a Blog?

Writing is thinking. By putting thoughts into words, I hope to:

1. Solidify my own understanding of topics
2. Share knowledge with others who might find it useful
3. Document my journey and look back on how far I've come

## Let's Connect

If you'd like to discuss any of the topics I write about, feel free to reach out via the [contact page](/contact).
